Our Story

Founded in 2026 in Polokwane,Limpopo; Ubuntu Animal Rescue was born out of passion to make a positive difference in the lives of homeless animals.

What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into a thriving rescue organization, saving hundreds of animals each year and finding them loving homes.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide shelter, medical care and love to every animal that comes through our doors.

We are proud of the impact we have made and are committed to continuing our mission of rescuing, rehabilitating and rehoming animals in need.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate animal lovers, dedicated volunteers and experienced professionals who are committed to our mission.

From rescuing animals in distress to providing medical care and finding them forever homes, our team works tirelessly to make a difference in the lives of the animals we serve.

We are grateful for the support of our community and the dedication of our team members who help us continue our important work.
